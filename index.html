About this Tool

This website is a tool specifically designed to help trainers explore and experiment with fusion combinations in the Pokémon Infinite Fusion game. It is an online resource that offers a user-friendly interface and a comprehensive set of features to enhance the fusion experience.

Why do some fused Pokémon without sprite?

Sprites Pack used by this tool are made by artists, from the Infinite Fusion Discord and Reddit, and some sprites are not drawn (some tools use Japeal generated sprites, but I don't do that!)

What is Pokemon Infinite Fusion

The Pokémon Infinite Fusion is centered around the captivating idea of merging two unique Pokémon species into a single, formidable entity. Trainers have the freedom to select any pair of Pokémon they wish, harnessing their individual strengths, abilities, and even physical appearances to forge a fusion Pokémon.

This extraordinary concept unlocks boundless possibilities, empowering trainers to strategize and explore unparalleled team compositions. By delving into the limitless potential of Pokémon Infinite Fusion, trainers gain a distinct competitive advantage and unveil uncharted realms of innovative battling strategies.

Advantages of this calculator

  1. Our Infinite Fusion Calculator uses the latest sprite packs and the most up-to-date Pokémon database to ensure accurate fusion predictions. You can trust the results of the calculator and avoid possible disappointment or confusion.
  2. Select an base Pokemon, and our calculator will generate the fusion result instantly. Witness the fusion of traits, abilities, and movesets come to life in a single click.
  3. Visualize your fusion creation with our visually stunning representations. See how your fusion Pokemon will look before implementing them in the game, ensuring they match your aesthetic preferences.
  4. Get access to detailed information about your fusion Pokemon, including base stats, types, abilities, and moves. Evaluate their strengths and weaknesses to build a well-balanced fusion team.

How to use Infinite Fusion

Follow these simple steps to begin using the Pokemon Infinite Fusion Calculator.

Step 1: Picking A Base Pokemon

Enter name or click random button to select the two base Pokemon. Select a Pokemon with the traits you want to keep in the fusion combo in the head.

Step 2: Examining Fusion Result

A list of possible fusion combinations is generated by the calculator after the base and fusion Pokemon have been chosen. Find the fusion that fits your desired qualities and strategic preferences by investigating the many options.
